At the boundary of one week and the next, the user wants to survey what's open, decide what matters this week, and stage it so Monday morning starts with momentum rather than overwhelm. The job is about reducing future cognitive load by paying a small upfront cost now.

## Tasks

1. Review open [[Task]]s across all [[Project]]s.
2. Mark which ones are this week vs. later, updating `due_at` or status on each.
3. Re-prioritize within the week — promote a few, demote the rest.
4. Schedule [[Reminder]]s for time-sensitive items.
5. Optionally archive or cancel [[Task]]s that no longer matter.

## Success criteria

- User closes the app with a clear mental image of the week.
- The Monday-morning view shows a small, finite set of tasks rather than the full backlog.
