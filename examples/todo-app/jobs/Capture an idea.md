Someone in the middle of a meeting, a walk, or another task has a thought worth keeping — a future todo, a follow-up, an idea. The job is to record it instantly, with as little friction as possible, and trust that it will resurface at the right moment so they can stop holding it in their head and return to what they were doing.

## Tasks

1. Trigger the capture surface — touches no entity directly (UI affordance).
2. Type or speak the thought into a new [[Task]].
3. Optionally tag it or assign a [[Project]] — but defer these; speed of capture matters more than completeness.
4. Dismiss the capture surface and return to the prior context.

## Success criteria

- Total time from "I had a thought" to "it's saved" is under 5 seconds.
- The thought appears later in the user's normal triage flow without them having to remember to look for it.
